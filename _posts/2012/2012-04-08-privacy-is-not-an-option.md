<blockquote>And as with the opt-in security settings of the past, today's opt-in privacy settings are leading to all sorts of problems. Every day we see headlines about privacy violations that could've been avoided if we used software that didn't treat privacy as an option.</blockquote>
<p>Fitting analogy for the state of technology today. What will it take for companies to make privacy a default, not an option?</p>
